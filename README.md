# Privacy Policy for Tapo

Last updated: July 10, 2026

## 1. Overview
Thank you for using Tapo ("the App"), a minimalist tempo and rhythm tool designed for music, breathwork, and fitness cadence training. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

## 2. Zero Data Collection
Tapo values your privacy above all else. 
- **No Personal Data:** The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal identifiable information, such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.
- **No Analytics & Tracking:** We do not use any third-party analytics tools, SDKs, or advertising frameworks. Your usage remains entirely private to you.
- **Local Operation:** All features, configurations, and tempo settings operate completely offline on your local device.

## 3. Device Permissions
- **Microphone Access:** The App may request microphone permission solely to provide local, real-time ambient audio visualizations (e.g., the audio-reactive pulse or wave effects). This audio data is processed instantly in memory on your device. It is **never** recorded, stored, or uploaded to any server.

## 4. Children's Privacy
Because Tapo does not collect any data, it is inherently safe for children and complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act).

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
